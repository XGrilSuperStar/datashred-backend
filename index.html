Why this needs to be ongoing

Why data brokers find you again

Deleting your info once isn't enough. Here's what actually keeps bringing it back.

01

Public records resync automatically

County records, voter files, and property listings feed broker databases on their own schedule — often monthly — regardless of past removals.

02

New brokers launch constantly

Dozens of new data broker sites register every year. A site that didn't have your info last month might have it today.

03

Brokers resell to each other

Even after you're removed from a broker's own site, that broker may have already sold a copy of your data to a different broker.
